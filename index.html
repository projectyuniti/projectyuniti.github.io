Installation

How to install?

A.R.I.S.E Sound System mods the existing sound libs and also adds new files to make your device sound awesome.

Preparation and some House keeping

Dirty flash your current rom according to rom dev's dirty flashing instructions if any other sound mod is currently installed. Make a copy of /system/build.prop as /system/build.prop.restore

Download

Download A.R.I.S.E. Sound System zip and tweakprop.zip(Optional).

Transfer

Transfer the downloaded files to your phone.

Flashing, the fun part

Reboot to your custom recovery and flash the A.R.I.S.E. Sound System zip and tweakprop.zip(Optional). And Enjoy the ultimate sound.
